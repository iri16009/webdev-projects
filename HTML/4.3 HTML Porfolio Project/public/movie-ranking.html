The Best Movies According to Gaby

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Parent Trap

I love how this movie showed 2 cultures and it also has one of the best soundtracks.

Harry Potter

Read all the books and watched all the movies. Books were better though. It's amazing how J.K. takes you to a completely different and magical world.

Taken

Amazing movie. It shows how our world is unsafe and how women are victims of abduction, even in places we think are safe.
